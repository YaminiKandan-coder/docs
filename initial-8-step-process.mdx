# Enable LIVEVUE (8-Step Onboarding)

The onboarding process activates LIVEVUE, generates a controlled test environment, and ensures templates and components are properly configured.

<Steps>

  <Step title="1. Start Initial Configuration">
    On the welcome screen, click **Next** to begin system setup.
  </Step>

  <Step title="2. Accept Terms & Generate Sample Data">
    - Accept terms and conditions  
    - Click **Create** to generate sample data and preview products  
    - Once complete, click **Next**

    <Callout type="tip">
      Sample data allows validation before publishing live storefront products.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="3. Select Your Business Role">
    Choose your operational role:
    - Artist  
    - Photographer  
    - Custom Framer  

    Click **Save**, then **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="4. Review Visualization Reference">
    Review the reference video to understand available visualization behaviors.

    Click **Next** to continue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="5. Create a Sample Component">
    Select the appropriate component:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Artist & Photographer">
        Select **Artwork Component** for pre-defined artwork products.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Custom Framer">
        Select **Upload Component** for customer-upload workflows.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    A popup window generates sample frame and mat configurations automatically.  
    Click **Save** to confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="6. Create & Enable LIVEVUE Template">
    - Click **Create** to generate the template  
    - Select **Enable**  
    - Confirm by clicking **Save**

    <Callout type="warning" title="Mandatory">
      The template must be enabled before linking components to products.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="7. Link Template & Validate Preview">
    - Use **Switch Template** to link the component to a product  
    - Click **Preview** to validate visualization and pricing behavior
  </Step>

  <Step title="8. Setup Complete">
    LIVEVUE is now active.

    You may proceed with advanced configuration or link custom components to live products.
  </Step>

</Steps>

# Glossary

<Accordion title="LIVEVUE">
The visualization engine within LIVEARf that renders frames, mats, and artwork previews in real time.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Component">
A configurable module that defines customization options, pricing behavior, and storefront visualization.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Template">
A configuration layer that connects components to Shopify products.
</Accordion>
