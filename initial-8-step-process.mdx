# 8 Step Initial Setup

This section guides you through enabling LIVEVUE and preparing your environment.

---

## Step 1: Start Initial Configuration

On the welcome screen, click **Next** to begin.

## Step 2: Accept Terms & Generate Sample Data

- Accept terms and conditions  
- Click **Create**  
- Sample data and preview products will be generated  
- Click **Next**

## Step 3: Select Your Business Role

Choose one:
- Artist  
- Photographer  
- Custom Framer  

Click **Save**, then **Next**.

## Step 4: Review Visualization Reference

Watch the provided reference video to understand visualization options.

Click **Next**.

## Step 5: Create a Sample Component

- Artists & Photographers → Select **Artwork Component**
- Custom Framers → Select **Upload Component**

Click **Save**.

## Step 6: Create and Enable Template

- Click **Create**
- Select **Enable**
- Click **Save**

## Step 7: Link Template and Preview

- Use **Switch Template**
- Link to a product
- Click **Preview**

## Step 8: Setup Complete

You are now ready to create custom components and link them to products.
